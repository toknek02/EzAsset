About AzAsset

AzAsset is a web-based Asset Management & Expiry Reminder System created to help organizations track important items and receive automatic expiry alerts.

The system centralizes all asset information and helps prevent missed renewals such as software licenses, subscriptions, equipment dates, and warranty expiration.

Objective

• Reduce manual tracking
• Improve organization productivity
• Prevent forgotten renewals
• Quick access to asset status
